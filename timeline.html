History of the Central Process Unit (CPU)

  • 1941
    Konrad Zuse develops the Z3, considered the world's first electromechanical computer.
  • 1945
    John Presper Eckert and John Mauchly create the Electronic Numerical Integrator and Computer (ENIAC), often considered the first general-purpose computer.
  • 1951
    UNIVAC I, the first commercially produced computer, is delivered to the U.S. Census Bureau.
