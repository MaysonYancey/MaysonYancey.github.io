Advanced RISC Machine (ARM)

ARM Holdings, headquartered in Cambridge, England, is a renowned designer of microprocessor architecture. Founded in 1990, ARM is known for creating energy-efficient and versatile processor designs that are licensed to other semiconductor companies. ARM's processor technology is widely used in various devices, including smartphones, tablets, and embedded systems. ARM processors are characterized by their power efficiency and scalability. They are designed for a wide range of applications, from low-power IoT devices to high-performance mobile and server systems. ARM's processor designs include the Cortex-A series for application processors, the Cortex-R series for real-time processors, and the Cortex-M series for microcontroller applications.
